About the Genesis Science Report

The Genesis Science Report (GSR) is a fast-paced, news-style television show exploring the connection between science and faith, delivering in-depth analysis of the latest scientific discoveries from a biblical worldview. 

We present compelling scientific evidence and discussions, challenging secular narratives and offering fresh perspectives rooted in biblical truth. 

Produced by David Rives Ministries, it airs on the Genesis Science Network, the only 24/7 science network with a biblical perspective.

Genesis Science Network

What to Expect

  • Engaging, dynamic discussion – Not a lecture or PowerPoint presentation.
  • 10-13 Minute Segment – Focused and structured conversation.
  • Live-to-tape Format – Filmed in one continuous session.
  • Broadcast-level production – Professional production team handling all graphics and transitions.
